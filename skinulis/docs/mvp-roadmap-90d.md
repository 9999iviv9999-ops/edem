# 90-day roadmap and KPI

## Phase 1 (days 1-30): launchable MVP

### Deliverables
- phone auth (OTP mock or provider)
- create campaign and pay for placement
- campaign feed + detail page
- quick micro-contribution checkout
- complaint flow
- payout request + hold logic

### KPI targets
- 300 paid publications
- >= 55% campaigns with at least one donation
- <= 8 abuse complaints per 100 campaigns

## Phase 2 (days 31-60): trust and conversion

### Deliverables
- campaign updates timeline
- post-factum reporting UI
- campaign scoring badge (basic)
- feed ranking improvements
- anti-spam heuristics tuning

### KPI targets
- +20% publication-to-first-donation conversion
- >= 25% repeat supporters
- payout hold false-positive rate < 15%

## Phase 3 (days 61-90): retention and unit economics

### Deliverables
- recurring support wallet
- supporter profile with contribution history
- pricing experiments (199/299/399)
- referral loop for supporters

### KPI targets
- CAC payback under 2 months
- >= 1.3 average paid placements per owner
- gross margin positive on paid placement line
