## Gap in current market

Most existing products are optimized for:
- creators/subscriptions
- large crowdfunding campaigns
- institutional charity

There is still a product gap for:
- small and urgent everyday goals
- contribution presets of 10-100 RUB
- "post now, get help quickly" UX
- no percentage commission model for campaign owner

## Product hypothesis for Skinulis

If users can publish with a low fixed fee and supporters can contribute in one tap with very small amounts, then:
- publication quality rises (paywall filters low-effort spam)
- trust rises with lightweight controls and visible reporting
- conversion improves due to tiny preset amounts

## Risks and mitigations

- Fraud bursts -> phone verification, active campaign limits, payout hold
- User distrust -> transparent campaign timeline and mandatory outcome report
- Low retention -> recurring "support wallet" and quick repeat payment flow
- Pricing mismatch -> A/B test 199 vs 299 RUB base tariff
