# Skinulis one-pager

## Idea

Skinulis is a platform for micro-help where many people contribute 10-100 RUB to urgent and everyday personal goals.

## Problem

Most requests for small financial help are posted in social networks or chats with:
- low trust
- no standardized reporting
- high fraud risk
- weak discoverability

## Solution

A dedicated marketplace with:
- paid campaign placement
- one-tap micro-contributions
- light anti-abuse controls (without heavy pre-moderation)
- transparent progress and post-factum reports

## Business model

- 299 RUB per campaign publication (14 days)
- 99 RUB feed bump option
- no commission from raised money in MVP

## Why this can scale

- low price point for campaign owners
- low cognitive load for supporters
- natural social sharing mechanics
- clear trust surface via reporting and complaint loop

## 90-day target

- 1,000 paid placements/month
- 55-65% campaigns with at least one donation
- abuse complaints <= 8 per 100 campaigns
